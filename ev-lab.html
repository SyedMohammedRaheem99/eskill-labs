Lab Overview

Our Electric Vehicle Lab is a comprehensive training facility that prepares students and professionals for the rapidly growing electric vehicle industry. The lab covers all aspects of EV technology from battery management to vehicle integration.

Designed in accordance with government standards and industry requirements, this lab provides hands-on experience with real-world EV systems and components. Students will gain practical knowledge in battery technology, motor control, charging systems, and vehicle diagnostics.

Technical Specifications

Space Requirements

  • Minimum: 1,500 sq ft (140 sq m)
  • Recommended: 2,000 sq ft (185 sq m)
  • Ceiling Height: 12 feet minimum
  • Loading capacity: 500 kg/sq m

Power Requirements

  • Main Supply: 3-Phase, 415V, 50Hz
  • Load Capacity: 50-75 kVA
  • UPS Backup: 10 kVA for critical systems
  • Emergency Power: Battery backup

Safety Features

  • Fire suppression system
  • Emergency shutdown switches
  • Ventilation and exhaust systems
  • Personal protective equipment

Environmental Controls

  • Temperature: 20-25°C
  • Humidity: 40-60% RH
  • Air quality monitoring
  • Proper lighting: 500 lux minimum
