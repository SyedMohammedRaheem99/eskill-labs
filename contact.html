Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to set up a technology lab?

Typically, a complete lab setup takes 4-8 weeks depending on the lab type, space requirements, and equipment availability. This includes consultation, design, procurement, installation, and training phases.

Do you provide training for our faculty?

Yes, comprehensive training is included in all our packages. We provide hands-on training for faculty on equipment operation, maintenance, curriculum integration, and student guidance.

What certifications do your labs provide?

Our labs are NSDC and ASDC aligned, providing students with industry-recognized certifications that enhance their employability in the respective technology sectors.

Do you offer post-installation support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive post-installation support including technical assistance, maintenance services, software updates, and periodic equipment health checks.

Can you customize labs according to our requirements?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating customized lab solutions based on your specific requirements, space constraints, budget, and educational objectives.

What is included in the lab package?

Our complete packages include equipment supply, installation, integration, faculty training, curriculum development support, documentation, and ongoing support services.
